SHRINK WRAPPING MACHINE

SHRINK SEAL PACKING MACHINE

The SHRINK SEAL PACKING MACHINE - (Tunnel Type) is for shrinkwrapping your product. This kind of packing wraps the product in a skintight PVC film cover. Making the product totally air tight & giving the packing the same shape as the product itself. Its applications also include sealing odd shaped articles, boxes, cones, rolls can be shrink wrapped with BOPP/ PVC / LDPE Film. The product is put into the PVC Sleeve & passed through the tunnel - the conveyor moves the product through the heat chamber. And the finished, sealed product comes out from the other side.

APPLICATION

  • Economical & fast packing - with range of usage for any type of article.
  • Easy to adjust the packing as per the size / shape of the product.
  • Machine can be moved around on castor wheels Tunnel is operated by DC Motor.
  • You can use low cost film also & even unskilled person can operate the machine.
